Project Description

Designed and implemented custom embedded applications across STM32 (STM8 to STM32H7), Atmel/ATmega, ESP32, and Infineon XMC platforms. Projects included LED chaser boards, tumbler machine automation, and integration of multiple circuits and sensors. Delivered optimized firmware with peripheral interfacing, low-power design, and real-time responsiveness. Ensured scalability and reliability for both prototype and production-level systems.
