Project Information

  • Project Title: Acoustic Box for Mobile Phone Privacy Protection
  • Role: Embedded Product & Hardware Designer
  • Category: Privacy & Security Products
  • Platforms: Custom Enclosure, Battery System
  • Project Type: Privacy & Security Product

Project Description

Developed a secure acoustic isolation box to prevent mobile phones from being tapped or eavesdropped on during confidential meetings. Designed with layered sound-dampening materials to block microphone pickup. Integrated dual 2600 mAh battery cells, providing over a week of backup power for portable operation. Delivered a compact, durable, and user-friendly solution for enhanced privacy and security.

Skills & Deliverables

  • Acoustic insulation design for sound isolation
  • Portable enclosure prototyping
  • Battery integration (dual 2600 mAh cells, >1 week life)
  • Low-power embedded system design
  • Privacy & security product development
